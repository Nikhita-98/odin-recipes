Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice.
  2. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  3. Cook the rice and chicken together.
  4. Add vegetables and cook until done.
