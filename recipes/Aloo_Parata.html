Aloo parata

Description

Aloo Parata is a popular Indian flatbread stuffed with spiced mashed potatoes. It's a delicious and filling dish, perfect for breakfast or any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes and mash them.
  2. Add spices to the mashed potatoes.
  3. Make dough with wheat flour and water.
  4. Roll out the dough and fill it with the potato mixture.
  5. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown.
