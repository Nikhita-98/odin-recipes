Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent as a variety of Persian pilaf. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, lamb, or fish).

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  3. Cook the rice and chicken separately.
  4. Layer the rice and chicken in a pot and cook on low heat.
  5. Serve hot with raita.
