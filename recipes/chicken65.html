Biryani

Description

Chicken 65 is a popular Indian dish made from small pieces of boneless chicken and served as an appetiser or snack

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Spices
  • Vegetables

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook rice separately.
  3. Layer chicken and rice in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat until done.
